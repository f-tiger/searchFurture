What makes a good hook?

A hook is the first line of a post — the one that decides whether anyone reads the rest. Great hooks create a small open loop in the reader's mind: a bold claim, a surprising number, a relatable pain, or a question they need answered. On fast-scrolling feeds, the hook does most of the work.

How to use this free hook generator

  1. Type your topic — what the post is actually about, in plain words.
  2. Pick the platform so the phrasing fits (a thread opener reads differently from a newsletter subject).
  3. Generate — you'll get a batch of openings across proven formulas (curiosity gap, hot take, story, listicle, and more). Copy the ones that fit and adapt them to your voice.
