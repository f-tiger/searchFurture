Privacy Policy

Brandloop is a pre-launch product. This page explains, in plain language, what we collect when you join the waitlist and how we handle it.

What we collect

  • Your email address when you join the waitlist.
  • Optional details you choose to share (what you do, your role, your biggest marketing headache).
  • Basic attribution — which link or channel referred you (e.g. a utm_source tag), so we know which outreach is useful.

Why we collect it

Only to contact you about the Brandloop launch and early access, and to understand demand. That's it.

How it's stored

Signups are stored in Cloudflare Workers KV on our infrastructure. We don't sell or rent your information, and we don't share it with third parties for their own marketing.

Cookies & local storage

We don't use advertising cookies. The site stores a small attribution value in your browser's local storage so a signup can be credited to the channel that referred you. You can clear it anytime via your browser settings.

Your choices

Want your information removed, or have a question? Email tuoqiantu@gmail.com and we'll delete your record. Every email we send will also include an unsubscribe option.

Changes

As the product launches, this policy may be updated. The latest version will always live at this page.
